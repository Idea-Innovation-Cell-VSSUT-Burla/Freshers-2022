Poetry

Utopia

With you
I want to be biased.
I want you to understand
My uncanny love for books.
The attachment I’ve developed
Over these years.
I want you to resonate with my feelings.
I want you to listen while I get crazy
Talking about my favorite troupe.
I want you to watch me skim through shelves
For hours, touching and feeling each page.
I want you to be patient with me
Listen me blabbering about the foolishness of the character.
I want you to hold my hands in silence
As I mourn the death of my dear protagonist.
I want you to read those long texts I send
Explaining my zeal when her long loved crush proposes her.
I want you to tell me over and again
“You look happier and sparkle around books”.
I want you to lay with me on the grass
As I read you a humorous fiction.
I want us to laugh through the book together
Lying under the shade of the maple.
Maybe forever is an illusion
I want us to be together for our little infinity.

Do you?

Do you wait for my messages like I do
Even though I know you would never text first.

Do you note the colour of my shirts
Or wish us wearing the same someday.

Do you look for the chance to lock eyes
Even for once, even for a second.

Do you get butterflies when I smile
The way I do, electric and genuine.

Do you open our chats and re-read them
To relive the thrill, smiles and emotions.

Do you notice the little things I say,
The way I know your constant emojis.

Do you feel shivers when I'm close
I do, is it infatuation or budding love?
